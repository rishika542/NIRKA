CULTURE AND VALUES

At NIRKA, we believe that our people are our greatest strength. We are committed to fostering a workplace that values diversity, equal opportunity, continuous learning, and clear, transparent guidelines. We know that diverse perspectives, ideas, and experiences drive innovation and better decision-making. That’s why we consider a wide range of qualities— skills, experiences, and potential—beyond just traditional qualifications. We encourage growth and learning, believing that the right mindset and passion for development are just as important as current skills. To support this, we have established clear guidelines that ensure fairness, transparency, and a positive work environment for everyone.
